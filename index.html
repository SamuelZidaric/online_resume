Welcome to Samuel Zidaric's Portfolio

Hello! I'm Samuel, a neuroscientist exploring the fascinating intersections between biological and artificial intelligence. When I'm not deep in data analysis, I'm searching for meaning in both my work and life. I created this space initially out of curiosity, but it's evolving into something more - a collection of professional accomplishments, yes, but also personal memories and reflections that matter to me. This website represents the ultimate tragedy of our time, the digital catastrophe that is unfolding before our eyes. We create this spaces knowing full well they are narcissistic voids, and yet, the true horror, we continue, hoping that someone will enjoy it.
