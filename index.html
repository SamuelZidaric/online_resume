Welcome to Samuel Zidaric's Portfolio

Hello! I'm Samuel, a neuroscientist navigating the fascinating paradox where biology meets computation. Is it not the supreme irony that we use artificial neural networks to understand actual neurons? In Trieste, I work at this precise contradiction—using algorithmic models to make sense of the very biological structures that inspired them. My research journey from C. elegans neurons to FAIR data principles represents not merely a career path but an ongoing dialectic between the material brain and its abstract outputs. When not contemplating this recursive loop of consciousness studying itself, I'm on basketball courts or immersed in art galleries—different frameworks for the same fundamental question: what patterns connect all things?
